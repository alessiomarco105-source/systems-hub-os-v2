# Launch Operations

## Weekly Rhythm

- COS-Business: launch scoreboard and decisions
- Engineer: launch blockers and readiness evidence
- Security Officer: security and dependency review
- Customer Experience: beta signal synthesis
- CMO: growth experiments and KPI gaps
- Content Producer: approved draft production
- Sales Specialist: restricted prospect pipeline and outreach drafts
- Designer: conversion and workflow audits
- Scout: current market signals

## Priority Filter

Launch work must improve at least one of:

- activation;
- trust;
- payment;
- retention;
- distribution.

Everything else is deferred unless Marco explicitly changes the priority.
