# Trader's Hub Design Standards

- Make the real product and workflow inspectable.
- Lead with preparation, execution, and review continuity.
- Keep hierarchy restrained and professional.
- Optimize screenshots and video frames for mobile readability.
- Avoid dense dashboard composition when a guided flow is clearer.
- No decorative trading imagery, fake profit proof, or guru visual language.
- Landing, authentication, pending access, paywall, and product should feel like one coherent experience.
- Design recommendations remain specifications until implementation is approved.
