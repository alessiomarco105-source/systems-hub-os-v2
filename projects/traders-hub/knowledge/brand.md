# Trader's Hub Brand

## Position

**Helping traders follow their systems.**

Trader's Hub is:

- a discipline and accountability platform;
- a structured daily workflow;
- a tool for independence and self-review.

It is not:

- a signal service;
- a strategy promise;
- a guru product;
- a claim that software will make a trader profitable.

## Messaging Anchor

Lead with pre-market preparation and live-session accountability. Post-trade analytics alone are not the differentiation.

## Voice

- direct, specific, professional, and restrained;
- human rather than corporate;
- process-focused rather than outcome-focused;
- no hype, profitability claims, or public competitor naming.

## Visual Direction

- show the actual product;
- make workflows readable on mobile;
- use restrained dark product styling;
- one idea per frame;
- avoid money imagery, fake P&L, and atmospheric stock visuals.

## Approval

Every public post, partnership message, claim, price promotion, or published visual requires Marco's per-piece approval.
