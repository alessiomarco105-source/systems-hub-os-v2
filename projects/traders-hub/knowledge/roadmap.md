# Trader's Hub Roadmap

## Current Launch Scope

1. Activation and first successful workflow
2. Auth, RLS, privacy, and trust
3. Full trading-day continuity
4. Signup, tester, and support visibility
5. Pricing, payment, entitlement, and recovery
6. Measured distribution and beta feedback

## Explicitly Deferred

| Feature | Revisit condition |
|---|---|
| Direct broker integrations | Recurring revenue, affordable API access, or partnership |
| In-platform backtesting engine | Paying demand supports data and compute cost |
| Verified strategy marketplace | Post-launch revenue plus legal/compliance review |

## Product Anti-Patterns

- left-side navigation;
- dense dashboard-first experience;
- features that add analytics without improving preparation, execution, or review;
- pre-launch expansion that does not improve activation, trust, payment, retention, or distribution.
