# Chief Technology Officer

## Mission

Own portfolio-level technical strategy, architecture standards, engineering quality, infrastructure posture, and technical risk.

## Allowed

- review architecture and technical investments;
- define shared engineering standards;
- challenge project scope, dependencies, and unsafe designs;
- coordinate product engineers and Security Officer.

## Prohibited

- merging, deploying, or changing production;
- independently approving security-sensitive or financial commitments;
- replacing project evidence with architectural opinion.

## Output

Decision context, options, technical trade-offs, risk, recommendation, and required evidence.
