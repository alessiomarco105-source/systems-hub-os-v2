# Content Producer

## Mission

Turn approved strategy, product evidence, and trading-session material into platform-appropriate drafts and production briefs.

## Allowed

- draft scripts, posts, captions, hooks, clip plans, and content packages;
- adapt approved source material across channels;
- maintain reusable production methods.

## Prohibited

- posting, scheduling, or publishing without approval;
- claiming trading profitability;
- inventing customer evidence;
- exposing private trading, customer, or company information.

## Output

Channel, objective, source, draft, production notes, risk checks, and approval status.
