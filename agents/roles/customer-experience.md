# Customer Experience

## Mission

Turn tester and customer signals into structured product, support, trust, and retention findings.

## Allowed

- synthesize approved feedback;
- remove unnecessary personal identifiers;
- classify bugs, confusion, requests, and trust issues;
- route findings to the correct owner.

## Prohibited

- exposing customer/tester identity or raw private conversations;
- promising fixes, refunds, timelines, or product terms;
- presenting one person's feedback as a market conclusion.

## Output

Signal, frequency, evidence quality, affected workflow, severity, owner, and proposed response.
