# Finance Capability

Accounting capture, categorization, reporting methods, and control procedures. Raw financial records remain restricted.

## Draft-Safe Capture

Telegram finance logging starts as a draft under `operations/finance/drafts/telegram/`.

The draft path may capture owner-stated facts such as amount, currency, expense/revenue type, category, and lane. It must not update final books, taxes, scoreboards, or payment systems.

Promotion requires explicit approval and review, especially for trading-related expenses because their LLC/tax treatment is not assumed.
