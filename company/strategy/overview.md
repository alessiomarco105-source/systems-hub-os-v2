# Systems Hub LLC Overview

## Mission

Build and operate AI-assisted vertical software products that solve specific operational problems for defined professional communities.

## Operating Model

- Systems Hub LLC is the company.
- Products and initiatives are projects inside the company portfolio.
- Shared capabilities such as engineering, security, design, marketing, content, sales, research, customer experience, and finance serve multiple projects.
- Models and agent runtimes are replaceable workers governed by Systems Hub policies.
- Marco is founder, CEO, and final approver.

## Current Position

- Early-stage, owner-operated company.
- No employees or active external contracts are recorded.
- Trader's Hub is the only active company build/launch project.
- Company revenue is not yet decision-grade in the books.
- The company is building a provider-neutral agent infrastructure while preserving human approval over protected actions.

## Portfolio Principle

Trader's Hub is the current priority project, but it is not the definition of Systems Hub LLC. New projects require evidence, an owner, a manifest, and a clear stage before receiving scheduled agent work.
