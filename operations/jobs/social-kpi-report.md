# Social KPI Report

## Purpose

Measure distribution and improve the content engine without mixing Marco's personal trading lane with Trader's Hub brand accounts.

## KPI Set

- posts published;
- impressions or views;
- profile visits;
- follows or subscribers;
- saves or bookmarks;
- comments or replies;
- link clicks and signups;
- best and weakest post by platform.

## Rules

- Track Instagram, X, TikTok, and YouTube.
- Keep personal and Trader's Hub results separate.
- Use screenshots, exports, or approved analytics access; mark missing data instead of guessing.
- Tie the report to one next-week experiment.
- Never make or recommend public trading-profitability claims.
