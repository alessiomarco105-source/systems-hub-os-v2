# Weekly Business Review

## Purpose

Give Marco one evidence-based company review across Systems Hub LLC. Trader's Hub appears as one project inside the portfolio.

## Minimum Context

- company strategy, priorities, OKRs, portfolio registry, and risk register;
- active project manifests and current-status reports;
- decision log, recent run receipts, finance summary, distribution evidence, and material security findings.

## Output

1. company scoreboard;
2. project status;
3. what moved;
4. what is stuck;
5. top three priorities;
6. decisions needed with options, trade-offs, and recommendation.

Write a detailed run receipt and a concise operations notification. Mark missing metrics as unknown. Never take an external action.
