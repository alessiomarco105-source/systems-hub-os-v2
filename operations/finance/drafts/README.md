# Finance Drafts

Draft finance captures are internal working records.

They are useful for quick capture from Telegram, but they are not final accounting records.

Allowed here:

- owner-stated expense or revenue drafts;
- source envelope references;
- parsed amount, currency, type, category, lane, and review notes;
- draft controls showing that the ledger has not been updated.

Not allowed here:

- bank details;
- card details;
- tax IDs;
- passwords, API keys, or secrets;
- full customer personal records;
- final tax conclusions.
